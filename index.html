Our Courses

D.G. Memorial Public School offers a wide range of courses designed to ignite curiosity and inspire excellence. Our curriculum is constantly updated to meet the needs of today’s world, preparing students for a successful future.

Our Kindergarten program focuses on building foundational skills through play-based learning. Students explore early literacy, numeracy, and creativity through interactive activities and guided learning.

In Classes 1 & 2, students begin to build core academic skills such as reading, writing, basic mathematics, and creative thinking. Activities like storytelling, basic science experiments, and art projects are also a key part of the curriculum.

At this stage, students strengthen their skills in subjects like Science, Math, and English while learning about history, geography, and the environment. Hands-on learning through experiments and field trips enhances their curiosity.

Students start focusing on more advanced topics, including introduction to computer science, social studies, and art history. This stage emphasizes problem-solving skills and creativity through projects and interactive learning.

In Classes 7 & 8, students are introduced to deeper concepts in Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies. Additionally, they explore elective subjects such as Music, Drama, and Design Technology.

At this level, students begin preparing for their board exams. Subjects include core Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), Social Science, and Languages. Students also begin to focus on their areas of interest with subject-specific training.

Senior Secondary students can choose from specialized streams: Science (with subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics), Commerce (with Business Studies, Accountancy, Economics), and Humanities (with History, Political Science, Psychology). This phase prepares them for college and competitive exams.
